Booking Online

Should you wish to book online, please follow the steps outlined below:

  1. Click on the button below, where you will be forwarded to My Grand Central.
  2. Depending on your activity, you will either sign in with your existing credentials, or register as a new user.
  3. Once logged in, locate Anna and list her as a favourite
  4. Your information will then automatically be sent to Anna and you will be able to book onto her diary directly from the app. This will also apply for future appointments.
