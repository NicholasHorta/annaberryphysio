About Anna Berry

Physiotherapist

Anna received her Bsc(Hons) Physiotherapy and Jean Blair Award for best clinical practitioner in 2013 from the University of the Witwaterstrand. She has undergone many courses to qualify in dry needling, dynamic and k - taping techniques, spinal and peripheral manipulation, foot biomechanics as well as clinical pilates. She has a passion for sports injuries after gaining experience working for a football club in London for 6 months. Her interests extend to treatment of back and joint pain. Anna is a registered member with the Health Professions Council of South Africa and the United Kingdom. Anna is also a member of the Sports injury special interest group and registered with the South African Society of Physiotherapy. Anna Berry is qualified in Sports Physiotherapy(SPT)

Our Rates

Anna Berry Physiotherapy is a cash practice(accepting cash, debit - and credit cards) with payment due after each session attended. Based on medical aid rates you will be able to claim the treatment costs from your respective medical aid. As far as possible the fees are charged within the prescribed medical aid rates but slight variations may occur depending on the nature of the intervention.

Your first consultation is charged at a set rate and includes a complex evaluation(to fully understand your symptoms) and a short treatment session. There after the consult fee lowers at a varying rate based on the nature of your condition and the high quality intervention required.

Physiotherapy fees are dependent on the required treatment modalities as described by the guidelines of the National Health Reference Price List(NHRPL) in accordance with the expertise of the therapist. Further details will be provided telephonically with the booking of your appointment
