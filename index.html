Our Services

Offering A diverse range of specialised treatment

Anna Berry Physiotherapy specialises in a wide range of medical services to get patients feeling healthier than ever before. And while we work hard to offer top-of-the-line treatments that help you feel better and get better, we also focus on preventative strategies to ensure you stay better for longer.


Contact us today for a full list of the specialty services we offer our patients

SPORTS PHYSIOTHERAPY

Acute Injury and Injury Prevention

Using various techniques from prevention, massage, k-taping and exercise to dry needling, muscle stimulations and manipulative techniques. Anna Berry Physio will get you back to your sport of choice in no time.

CHRONIC PAIN

Pain Management

If you're suffering from arthritis, fibromyalgia, pain post surgery or other chronic conditions. Anna Berry Physiotherapy offers many techniques to help manage your pain and help ensure a better quality of life.

RESPIRATORY THERAPY

Relief from Acute and Chronic Respiratory Conditions

If suffering from acute bronchitis, pneumonia, pleurisy, sinus or other cardiopulmonary conditions. Physiotherapy has been proven to assist with the mobilisation of secretions and improve shortness of breath associated with these conditions to facilitate a quicker recovery

PAEDIATRIC PHYSIOTHERAPY

Bespoke Quality Care

Offering screening for developmental delay, improving co-ordination and gross motor skills as well as nebulisation and chest physiotherapy for respiratory conditions acute or chronic.

POST-OP PHYSIOTHERAPY

To improve quality of life

Ensuring you get up and about as soon as possible post-surgery. Whether its post- cardiac, orthopaedic, maxillofacial or general surgery; body strengthening, pain relief techniques and return to activities of daily living are not only important but necessary for full recovery. Contact us for home visit, if required.

NEUROLOGICAL PHYSIOTHERAPY

Rehabilitation

Improve strength and function post stroke, traumatic brain injury, meningitis, Guillian-Barre, spinal cord injury or other neurological disorders. We offer subacute and chronic physiotherapy help regain independence.
